Hi, I'm Krispin
Analyst | Developer | Engineer

Start

I'm an engineer with a background in mechanical and quality engineering, programming, and data analysis. Graduating from the University of Wollongong with a double degree in engineering and mathematics, I'm all about applying my wide knowledge base to develop holistic solutions - from identifying process improvement opportunities to engaging in product design, my main goal is to ensure that anything I output is both soundly designed and cost effective.

I am:


    • I am always for learning new things - having a working knowledge of the system you are trying to improve is essential to designing your part to work with the whole

    • I love automation and efficiency - I believe there are always ways to improve processes and workflow, and I'm passionate about discovering them.


    • I have good ideas; other people have good ideas too. I like to keep the end goal in mind when working with others on a project - when someone comes up with an idea that may be an improvement to mine, I'm on-board with testing it out and working with them to achieve that goal

    • I work well with steep learning curves - I proved this through having picked up machine learning and Power FX as part of a data analysis and quality improvement project I worked on whilst with King Living

    • I have a wide range of interests - this is especially useful for facing complex problems requiring multiple disciplines


    • I can remove distractions and work under pressure when required; I generally prefer to work with hard deadlines, allowing me to prioritize more easily.


    • I am detail-oriented - whether reviewing engineering drawings or code, when tasked with editing I analyse everything, and pride myself on accuracy. In doing this, I have found through my work that I prefer working using a Git workflow: write, analyse/review, update, aiming for a low response time when I need to review something


    • As a father of two, I've learned that I am highly patient - I appreciate that differences in opinion on how best to move forward can happen, and can work with a variety of personalities daily, collaborating on projects and in general business.

Here's a collection of the projects I have worked on (or am currently working on):

Campbelltown Business Dashboard

An exploratory dashboard representing the data from the Campbelltown Business Data API.

One possible conclusion/result from the data presented could be that there are still businesses with little to no web presence - for example, they may benefit from a website and become the target of certain marketing campaigns.

Registered Engineers Lookup

A lookup tool for engineering grads & undergrads combining the data from the site EngineeringCompanies.com.au and the goverment data API.

It is difficult to find business contacts, specifically engineer contacts, to approach for information about working in the engineering field. This tool uses freely available information to link companies that offer grad roles with engineers registered with Engineers Australia (using the Registered Engineers of Queensland dataset).

Collapsible 20T Shop Press

Tooling designed and built by me, for home use (press capacity is 20T). Link to the design is here: Collapsible press in Onshape.

I needed a press for working with bearings, and decided that this would make a good design and build project - I've cut, drilled, and welded it into its current state.
